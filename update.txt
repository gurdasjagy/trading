exit_threshold_sigma: f64 (default 0.5 standard deviations - close when spread reverts to within 0.5 sigma)

When spread reverts to within exit_threshold_sigma * std_dev of the mean: close both legs
Hard stop: if spread moves to 4 sigma against the position, close both legs (stop loss)
Time stop: close after 4 hours if spread hasn't reverted
